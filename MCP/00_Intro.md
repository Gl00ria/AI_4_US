#### What is the **Model Context Protocol (MCP)**?
A standardized framework by "Anthropic"" that enables AI models to connect with external tools and data sources, providing secure, scalable and real time access. Think of it as a bridge.


#### Security & Permissions:
- The Connection is specific to only what the connector needs
- It sees what you see



#### Consider before using MCP:
- What tool is the most valuable to your daily work to connect?
- What tasks you can just copy/paste that the connector can do automatically?
- Is combining multiple connectors/MCPs will save you significant time?
