
#### What is AI Fluency?
Is the ability to engage with AI systems in an (Effective, Efficient, Ethical & Safe). In order to that, you have to "Think About AI" to learn "How To Think With AI".


#### Components of AI Fluency?
- **Delegation:** When should the Human do the work, and when the AI should.
- **Description:** How to communicate with AI systems.
- **Discernment:** How to evaluate with the AI gives.
- **Diligence:** How to ensure your interaction with AI is (responsible, Transparent & Accountable).
