#### What GPT stands for?

It stands for **G**enerative **Pre**-trained **T**ransformer.
it can generate human like text & can continue the text in a contextually appropriate manner.

#### What is GPT?

A pre-trained Model that uses the [Transformer Architecture](https://github.com/Gl00ria/AI_4_US/blob/main/00_Warm_Up/04_Neural_Network_Architecture.md) to process and understand Natural Language. GPT Models are **Autoregressive (Left to Right)**, which means they generate text by predicting the next word in a sentence based on the proceeding word.

#### What GPT good at?

- Text completion.
- Dialog generation.
- Content creation.
