# Large Language Models

**LLMs** are models that can process human language, and generate like responses.

#### What are the main features to LLMs?

1. Larger compared to other models.
2. General purpose.
3. Ability to be Pre-trained & Fine-tuned.

#### LLM parameters:

The larger number of parameters the model has, the better it can understand & work with language. Think of parameters like a building blocks, and yes we are talking about Millions, Billions or even Trillions of building blocks.
